What are the Training/Educational Requirements Needed to be a Veterinary Nurse?

There are multiple ways of becoming a certified Veterinary Nurse

The first way you could become a Vet Nurse is by:

  • Getting a level 3 diploma in Vet Nursing
  • Must have 5 GCSEs at grade C and above, which must include English language, maths and a science subject.
  • An animal nursing assistant (ANA) or veterinary care assistant (VCA) qualification, along with functional skills level two in application of number and communication.
  • Pass written multiple choice question exams in the theory of veterinary nursing and pass a practical exam in veterinary nursing skills.
