The Benefits and Drawbacks of being a Veterinary Nurse


Benefits
Lots of job offers
Satisfaction of helping the needy, sick animals
You do not get sued as much as doctors do
You get paid very well

Drawbacks
Intense training/school is needed to become a vet
Some encounters with pets can be difficult because they may not be trained well
Giving euthanasia to an animal may be heart-breaking
You are constantly on-call
