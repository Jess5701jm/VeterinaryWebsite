What it is like to be a Veterinary Nurse

What Do You Do?

According to an interview with Katy Finbow, her main job is to watch over the animals that are under anesthetics during a surgical procedure. If there is anything wrong during the procedure, she would be the person who would try to fix the problem. (Of course with the help of the doctors. Every job has team effort.) She is also the main person who cleans up the mess after the procedure is done. That would consist of throwing away needles, disinfecting certain equipments, and cleaning the table on which they did the procedure on.
